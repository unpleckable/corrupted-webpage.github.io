What is Corrupted Lands

A STORY TOLD
THROUGH MINECRAFT

Corrupted Lands is a fully scripted, cinematic Minecraft SMP. Think less survival server — think more collaborative TV show. Writers plan arcs. Players are cast. Events are orchestrated. Every episode is a new chapter in an evolving story.

Inspired by series like Unstable Universe, we take the format further: faction politics, betrayals, custom weapons, mass-PvP battles, and plot twists that nobody sees coming — not even most of the cast.

Every character has a role. Every death matters. And when the server ends — the story is complete.
